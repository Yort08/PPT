Reorganizes content scopes to adapt to weather disruptions, assemblies, or pacing shifts.

Daily Plan (Micro Action Guides)

Immediate classroom concerns, scripts, questions, and last-minute room adjustments.

Formats Comparison

Instructor: Esteban // Brief, Semi-Detailed, and Detailed formats
Format Key Features Target
Brief Outline format, targets seasoned teachers, copies objectives/subject matter/evaluation/assignment, but omits detailed steps. Seasoned Teachers
Semi-detailed Shorter format, copies all steps but omits student answers. General Faculty
Detailed (DLP) Full-scale roadmap for pre-service teachers. Explicitly split into two dynamic columns: "Teacher's Activity (TA)" vs. "Students' Activity (SA)". Pre-Service / Practicum

DLP Script Columns

Detailed Lesson Plans (DLP) partition classroom dialogue. Click a phase:

Teacher's Activity (TA)

Students' Activity (SA)

"Good morning, Teacher! (Students stand up and align desks)"

Module 6 Lesson Plan

Instructors: All Members // Teaching Session Sheet

📋 Plan Profile & Objectives

Subject: Principles & Methods of Teaching (PMT)
Time Frame: 1 Hour & 30 Minutes (90 Minutes)
Topic: Module 6: Management of Instruction (Planning & Congruence)

I. Objectives

  • Explain the importance and purposes of instructional planning.
  • Apply the Rule of Congruence to align objectives, methods, and evaluation.
  • Compare Brief, Semi-Detailed, and Detailed lesson plans.

II. Subject Matter

Reference: GEE-PMT Module 6 Handout
Materials: Chalkboard slides, Congruence Wheel diagram, quiz templates.

👣 III. Learning Procedures (90 Mins)

  • A. Routines & Schema (15 mins): Desk alignment check, daily prayer, checking attendance, and short schema activation review.
  • B. Motivation & Reflection (10 mins): Ask: *"What happens if a teacher conducts a session without any roadmap?"*
  • C. Lesson Proper & Discussion (40 mins):
    • Discuss the purposes and definitions of instruction planning.
    • Demonstrate Objectives & Assessment alignment on the Congruence Wheel.
    • Contrast Brief vs. Semi-Detailed vs. Detailed (DLP) layouts.
    • Formulate SMART goals in Cognitive, Affective, and Psychomotor domains.
  • D. Alignment Practice (15 mins): Students perform group alignment exercises mapping objectives to methods and evaluation types.
  • E. Evaluation & Wrap-Up (10 mins): Administer 10-Item Identification Quiz and discuss homework/assignment parameters.
